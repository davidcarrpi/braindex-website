Delete Your Account

Last updated May 16, 2026

In one line

Open Braindex on your phone, go to Settings → Delete account, and tap Delete. Your profile and account data are removed immediately. If you can’t access the app, email us at david@movietoons.co and we’ll delete your data within 30 days.

What is Braindex?

Braindex is a daily AI vocabulary game published by Braindex on the Apple App Store and Google Play Store. This page explains how to permanently delete your Braindex account and the data we hold about you.

How to delete your account in the app

  1. Open Braindex on your phone (iOS or Android).
  2. Tap the person icon in the top-right of the home screen to open Settings.
  3. Scroll to the bottom of Settings.
  4. Tap Delete account (shown in red).
  5. Confirm the prompt. Your account and the data listed below are deleted immediately.

You don’t need to email us — the in-app delete is the fastest and most direct path.

How to request deletion if you can’t access the app

If you’ve uninstalled the app or can’t sign in, email us from the address tied to your Braindex account:

Braindex Privacy

Email: david@movietoons.co

Subject: Delete my Braindex account

Include in the email:

  • The email address you used to sign in to Braindex (Apple, Google, or email/password)
  • Your in-app display name, if you remember it
  • Approximate date you started using Braindex (helps us locate the account if you used a private-relay Apple ID)

We will process the request and confirm by reply within 30 days (typically within 1–3 business days).

What is deleted, and what is kept

Data type What happens on deletion Retention if any
Account profile
(display name, email, sign-in provider, account creation date)		 Permanently deleted. None.
Username reservation
(if you set a custom unique username)		 Released. The username becomes available for someone else to claim. None.
Authentication record
(Firebase Auth user + OAuth tokens)		 Permanently deleted. If you signed in with Apple, the Apple authorization token is also revoked automatically. None.
Optional profile answers
(age range, gender, student level, “why Braindex”, plus — for adults — optional email, phone, AI-Zoom-call preference)		 Permanently deleted alongside the account record. None.
Daily leaderboard entries
(scores you submitted on past days)		 Past entries are not auto-removed — this keeps the historical leaderboard intact. They stay attached to the display name you played under, not to your private identity. You can ask us to anonymise or remove specific entries by emailing david@movietoons.co. Kept under the display name until you request removal.
Top-streaks leaderboard entry
(your current streak + display name)		 Not auto-removed for the same reason as daily scores. Email us to anonymise or remove it. Kept under the display name until you request removal.
Per-word right/wrong logs
(which AI terms you got right or wrong, used internally to tune future puzzles)		 Not auto-removed. These are only readable by us, never by other players. Email us to remove them along with your account. Kept until you request removal.
Feedback messages
(anything you submitted via the in-app feedback form)		 Not auto-removed when the account is deleted. The link from each message to your user identifier becomes orphaned the moment the account record is gone. Held until we’ve acted on it, then archived. Email us to remove specific messages sooner.
Newsletter signup
(if you entered your email on braindexapp.com’s “Get notified at launch” form)		 Not deleted automatically by the in-app account delete — the newsletter list is a separate website-side record. Email us to remove it. Held until launch emails are sent, or until you ask us to remove it.
On-device data
(reminder time, cached daily session, locally stored preferences)		 Removed automatically the next time you open the app after deletion. Uninstalling the app removes all of it immediately. None — stored only on your device.

For complete erasure — including leaderboard rows, streak entries, per-word logs, feedback, and any newsletter record — the cleanest path is: (1) delete your account in-app to wipe your profile and revoke your Apple token immediately, then (2) email david@movietoons.co asking us to remove the remaining records. We process those within 30 days.

Apple Sign-In specifically

If you signed in with Apple, deleting your account inside Braindex also revokes the authorization token Apple issued for our app, per Apple App Store guideline 5.1.1(v). You will no longer see Braindex listed under Settings → Apple ID → Sign in with Apple on your iPhone after a few minutes.

Children’s data

Braindex does not request email or phone number from users who indicate they are under 18. If you are a parent or guardian and want a child’s account deleted, email david@movietoons.co with the child’s in-app display name and any other identifying details. We will delete the account within 30 days.

Legal & further reading

Account deletion rights are also covered in our Privacy Policy. If you have questions or want to escalate, email david@movietoons.co.
