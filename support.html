Support

Last updated May 16, 2026

Quickest path

The fastest way to reach us is the in-app feedback chat icon on the home screen of Braindex. For anything we can’t solve in the app — or if you can’t open the app — email david@movietoons.co. We reply within 1–3 business days.

1. Report a bug or request a feature

Send feedback from inside the app

Open Braindex on your phone → tap the chat bubble icon at the top of the home screen → type your message + optional 1–5 star rating → Send.

We read every message and use them to prioritise the roadmap.

Or email us

If you can’t open the app or want a longer conversation: david@movietoons.co.

2. Request data changes (without deleting your account)

You can ask us to delete or change specific pieces of data we hold about you while keeping your account active. Examples:

  • Remove your email address from your account profile
  • Remove your phone number from your account profile
  • Delete the optional profile answers you filled in (age range, gender, student level, why-Braindex, AI Zoom call interest)
  • Remove a specific past leaderboard entry
  • Delete the feedback messages you have submitted
  • Correct an inaccurate display name or other field

How to send the request

Email david@movietoons.co with the subject line “Data change request”. Use this template — tap to copy the text into your email:

Subject: Data change request

Hi Braindex,

Please make the following changes to my data:
  [ ] Remove my email address from my profile
  [ ] Remove my phone number from my profile
  [ ] Delete my optional profile answers (age, gender, etc.)
  [ ] Remove a specific leaderboard entry (date: __________ )
  [ ] Delete my feedback messages
  [ ] Other: __________

To identify my account:
  - Sign-in email or Apple/Google account email: __________
  - In-app display name: __________

Thanks.

We process requests within 30 days (usually 1–3 business days) and confirm by reply.

Some on-device data (your local streak count, daily reminder time, cached session) is stored only on your phone and can be cleared by uninstalling the app — no email needed.

3. Delete your entire account

If you want to delete everything — your account, profile data, and all associated records — the in-app delete is fastest:

  • Open Braindex → Settings (top-right person icon) → Delete account.

Full instructions and a breakdown of what is deleted vs retained are on the Delete your account page.

4. Privacy questions or complaints

For anything privacy-related — questions about how we handle your data, GDPR / CCPA requests, complaints — email david@movietoons.co. Full details are in our Privacy Policy.

5. Children’s data (under 13)

Braindex does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and want to review, change, or delete data we may have collected from your child, email david@movietoons.co. We will respond within 30 days.
