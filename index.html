A note from the founder

Hi, I'm David. I built Braindex because the AI world is moving faster than anyone can read about it, and most people I talk to feel a step behind. I wanted something tiny and daily — the way Wordle made vocabulary fun — that could quietly turn anyone into someone who understands the AI conversation, three minutes at a time.

The goal is simple: one year of daily play and you can hold your own in any conversation about modern AI. Whether you're a student, a marketer, a curious parent, or working in tech and tired of pretending you know what RAG means — this is for you.
